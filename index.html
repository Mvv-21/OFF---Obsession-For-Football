What is OFF?

OFF stands for Obsession for football. It is an app for training or learning football online. It does not matter if your objective in football is taking lessons either online with the computer teaching you or learning with an actual trainer online. Transactions from student to trainer can be done trough the app. People using OFF have got far in their football career. Many of the students using OFF have got a professional work in football.

Why use OFF?

With OFF, you can train Online or if you are a trainer, you can also win money with OFF. If you do not want to receive classes from a trainer, you can get the classes from the app's tutorials. It includes exercises to be fit, techniques to play football, theorical lessons. You can try it for free, and if you like it, you get access to the rest of the lessons. Even if OFF is an app it can be used for professional training and many football organisations reccomend it.

About levels

All levels are available here in OFF. You can start from not knowing even how to run properly to become a professional. Don't worry if you don't have the basics you can choose between the different levels that we have. There are 4 levels: needs improving, developing, good, excellent, obviously there will be trainers that see your training and guide you what level you should take. Also there are all of these levels available for all ages. You can choose if you want classes individually or in a small group, depending on what you want to get better on.

About locations

OFF is an online app with videos and where you can chat with our trainers but if you prefer to attend presential training lessons then we have several training camps all over the world but Africa. You can assist classes in these locations. We have the greatest number of training camps in Spain, with 69 camps.
